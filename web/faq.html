    FAQ's

    What is Gift of a kind?
    Giftofakind.com is India's multi-category online gifting company, providing one of the best curated collection of gifts, flowers, cakes & personalized products for all personal occasions & festivals (both domestic & international).
    What kind of products and gifts are available at Giftofakind.com?
    Giftofakind.com is a one-stop shop for all kinds of gifting requirement. We have created bespoke collections for almost all kinds of recipients & occasions. We handpick items from over 200 product categories. We are also market leaders in specialty product/gift categories like Personalized products, Hampers, Flowers, Cakes and Handmade chocolates. We also offer large signature collections of over 2000 products for all key festivals in India, including both domestic (Rakhi, Diwali, Bhaidooj ,etc) and international (valentine’s day, mother’s day, etc).
    Where can Giftofakind deliver products?
    Giftofakind.com delivers its products worldwide. We ship our products to around 20,000 pincodes in India and around 200 countries worldwide. Apart from our own delivery network, we use premium courier partners such as DHL, Blue Dart, FedEx, etc for our domestic & international deliveries. We can usually delivery domestic orders in 2-5 business days and international orders in 4-7 business days to regular pincodes. It may take up to 10-12 business days to deliver products to remote areas.
    How can I buy an item from Giftofakind.com?
    Giftofakind only takes online orders through its website Giftofakind.com. We do not take any order on phone or via email and we do not have retail outlets. Alternatively, you cancontact usanytime and we’ll get back to you ASAP.
    Will my gift look exactly as it is seen on the website?

    In case of flowers and cakes, pictures are representative of the composition of the product and in certain cases, the design of the actual product may differ from what is shown on the website. We currently have a large vendor base for flowers & cakes across India and while they strive hard to replicate designs on our website as far as possible, there might be variations at times, particularly in smaller towns where production equipment & tools are limited.

    All other products are delivered as shown on the website. As most of our catalog is made up of unique and designer products, the quantity is always limited. Hence, in case, we run out of the product you ordered, we will get in touch with you to help you with a suitable alternative.

    Do you gift wrap or provide packaging options?
    We currently do not provide a gift wrap option. But we provide an option to add a gift box at a nominal charge (the option is available in the shopping cart). We currently have over 200 designs of gift boxes in various sizes and shapes to suit different occasions & gift items. Hence, if you decide to add a gift box, rest assured that the recipient will have a delightful experience unboxing your gift.
    Do you deliver all products everywhere?
    We deliver products to most of the places in the world, but not everywhere. We deliver to more than 20,000 pincodes in India and to more than 200 countries internationally. We deliver our products to all regular pincodes in India and abroad; however, we do not deliver our products to extremely remote locations. Also, the deliverability of the product depends on the kind of product you are buying. You can enter your pincode or country at the product page or during checkout to check shipping availability & tentative delivery
    What date should I select when ordering for midnight delivery?
    Midnight & fixed time deliveries should be placed 24 hours in advance. If you want to surprise your loved one at 12:01 am on 14th, then you need to select 13th as the delivery date and Midnight delivery as the delivery.
    Do you provide a Cash on Delivery (COD) option?
    No, we do not provide COD option for a simple reason that the sender of the gift (who buys the gift & is responsible for the payment) is invariably different from the recipient of the gift.
