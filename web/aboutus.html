    About Us

    Giftofakind.com is India’s largest multi-category gifting company, providing one of the best curated collection of gifts, flowers, cakes &personalized products for all personal occasions & festivals. The firm has a global footprint with almost half of its orders originating from outside of India and almost one-third of its orders getting delivered internationally to more than 90 countries across the globe. Giftofakind has brought to consumers India’s Gift Discovery platform, taking a massive leap forward in addressing consumers’ gifting needs.
